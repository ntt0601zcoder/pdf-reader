# PDF Reader

A clean, distraction-free reader for your PDFs — built for long reading sessions.

## 👉 Try it live: **[pdf-reader.thuannt.id.vn](https://pdf-reader.thuannt.id.vn)**

---

## What it does

- 📂 Open PDFs from your Google Drive, or straight from your device.
- 🌙 Read comfortably with **Light**, **Sepia**, and **Dark** themes — easy on the eyes at night.
- 🖍️ **Highlight** text in multiple colors.
- 📝 Attach **notes** to your highlights, and export them to Markdown.
- 🔖 **Bookmark** pages and jump back to them anytime.
- 📖 Remembers the **last page you read** and takes you right back next time.
- 🔎 **Full-text search** across the whole document.
- 🗂️ Browse the document **outline** to jump between sections.
- ☁️ Your highlights, notes and bookmarks **sync to your Google Drive**, so they
  follow you across devices and browsers.
- 🌐 Available in **Vietnamese** and **English**.

---

## Privacy

Your files are read in your own browser. Highlights, notes and bookmarks are
saved to your own storage — there is no separate server collecting your data.
